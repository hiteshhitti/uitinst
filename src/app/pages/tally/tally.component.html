Tally ERP 9 with GST Course

This course is designed to provide you with a comprehensive understanding of Tally ERP 9, a leading accounting software. Learn accounting principles, GST features, financial reporting, inventory management, and much more in this detailed course.

account_balance What You'll Learn
  • check_circle
    Introduction to Tally ERP 9:
    Get familiar with the Tally interface and understand its basic features. Learn how to create a company, configure the system, and set up essential parameters for efficient use of the software.
  • check_circle
    Basic Accounting Concepts:
    Learn the fundamentals of accounting including ledgers, journal entries, trial balance, and the concept of debit and credit. Understand accounting terminology and its real-world application.
  • check_circle
    Voucher Entry in Tally:
    Master the process of voucher creation in Tally for various transactions like sales, purchase, receipts, payments, and journal entries. Learn to maintain proper records for daily accounting activities.
  • check_circle
    Inventory Management:
    Learn how to manage inventory, record stock entries, track sales, purchases, and stock movement. Get hands-on with stock categories, batches, and units of measurement.
  • check_circle
    GST in Tally:
    Understand the GST structure and how to apply GST in Tally. Learn to create GST-enabled vouchers, generate GST returns, and maintain compliance with government regulations.
  • check_circle
    GST Reports and Returns:
    Learn to generate GST reports such as GST Sales, GST Purchase, and GST Tax Liability. Understand the process of filing GST returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B) in Tally and maintaining accurate records for taxation.
  • check_circle
    Financial Statements in Tally:
    Learn how to generate important financial reports like Profit & Loss statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow statement, and Bank Reconciliation statement. Understand how to analyze financial performance using Tally reports.
  • check_circle
    Payroll and Employee Management:
    Get hands-on with Tally's payroll features. Learn how to maintain employee records, calculate salaries, and generate payslips. Understand statutory compliance such as PF, ESI, and tax deductions.
  • check_circle
    Banking and Cash Management:
    Learn to manage bank accounts and cash transactions. Understand how to record bank payments, receipts, reconcile bank statements, and generate bank-wise reports in Tally.
  • check_circle
    Multi-Currency Accounting:
    Learn how to deal with multi-currency transactions in Tally. Manage exchange rates, foreign currency purchases/sales, and generate reports for international business transactions.
  • check_circle
    Cost Center and Profit Center Accounting:
    Understand how to use cost centers and profit centers in Tally to track expenses and revenues by different departments, projects, or business units for better decision-making.
  • check_circle
    Advanced Features:
    Learn advanced Tally features like Auto Bank Reconciliation, Data Import/Export, Tally Security, and customizing reports according to your business needs.
  • check_circle
    Practical Projects:
    Work on practical projects like creating a complete accounting system for a small business, maintaining financial records, applying GST, and generating financial reports for clients.
